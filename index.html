A CITY THAT TALKS TO YOU

Mission

Polis’ vision is to create a city that speaks through Bluetooth beacon networks. We aim to bridge the gap between the online and physical world by bringing context into the way we interact with the internet. The Polis app is built around trimming down beacon-related software on the consumer’s phone. Rather than having vendor-specific apps, Polis universalizes all the content different companies want to provide under one single application. Individuals simply need to register their beacon on the Polis network and the content can be broadcasted to anyone with the app. The goal is to minimize the effort required to utilize the physical context of the digital world.

How Do iBeacons Work

iBeacons send out packets of information from itself to an ios or android device. This allows for location-based info to be passed to the user without and invasive scan or search to do it.

Cost Effective

iBeacons 60-80% cheaper than traditional Bluetooth. That means that you can place more of them and provide more information.
